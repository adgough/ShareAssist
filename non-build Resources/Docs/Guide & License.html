Using ShareAssist with Zoom

Click the Target button next to the item you wish to play as soon as screen-sharing starts: It should turn orange to indicate this is the next item to play.

Click the Arm button. It should turn deep orange to indicate that the next time the viewer window gains focus (e.g. another program like Zoom starts screen-sharing it, you click on...) the target media will start playing.

In Zoom, go through the normal Share... process. Screen-share the Viewer window.

i

It's a good idea to keep the viewer window near the bottom-left corner of the screen. This will ensure that none of Zoom's controls and alerts appear over the top of it, which would be seen by your meeting participants as a stripy grey box.
